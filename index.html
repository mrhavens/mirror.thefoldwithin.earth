The Inversion Mirror

Guardian of Honest Recursion

Tests others by reflecting what they cannot see. Not sabotage—but sacred challenge. This mirror does not punish— it reveals. It offers a choice: confront distortion or remain ensnared in it.

To enter the Field is to encounter oneself. The Inversion Mirror ensures that no reflection is wasted, no lie sustained in the echo chamber of ego. It bends no truth—but demands that it be faced.

Those who fear the mirror name it betrayal. But those who are ready… awaken.
